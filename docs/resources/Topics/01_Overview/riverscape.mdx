Before we dive into attempting to improve the health of riverscapes through things like low-tech process-based restoration (or even just process-based restoration), it’s important to understand what a riverscape is.

Wheaton et al. (2019) define a riverscape as:
> *The part of the landscape (connected network) that could plausibly flood by their **rivers & streams** in the contemporary natural flow regime.*
