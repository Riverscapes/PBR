# Fluvial Geomorphology: Reading Riverscapes

This semester-long online asynchronous course focuses on building your skills and confidence in reading landscapes with a particular focus on riverscapes. You will learn how to look at any riverscape and be able to map and interpret its character and form, while making informed inferences about its behavior and the processes shaping it.

More course info and registration on the [course specific website](https://riverscapes.github.io/Fluvial-Geomorphology/syllabus/2026Spring)
