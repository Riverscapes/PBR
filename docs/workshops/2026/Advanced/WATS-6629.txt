## Course Information



|**Course Title** | **Course Number** | **Credits** |**Term** | 
|----|----|----|----|
  Screening & Prioritization of Riverscape Restoration| WATS/CEWA 6629| 1 Graduate Credit (1.5 CEUs) - 20 contact hours| Fall 2025|

### [Catalog Description](https://usu.catalog.acalog.com/content.php?filter%5B27%5D=WATS&filter%5B29%5D=6629&filter%5Bcourse_type%5D=-1&filter%5Bkeyword%5D=&filter%5B32%5D=1&filter%5Bcpage%5D=1&cur_cat_oid=40&expand=&navoid=30491&search_database=Filter#acalog_template_course_filter)


This course is an advanced geospatial analysis of riverscape network models for purposes of screening, filtering, contextualizing and prioritizing conservation and restoration actions.

<details closed>

### Prerequisite Recommendations
Students are recommended to be proficient in GIS (See: [getting started in QGIS](https://riverscapes.freshdesk.com/support/solutions/articles/153000083973-getting-oriented-in-qgis) for self-paced; see [USU's Geospatial Collective](https://www.usu.edu/geospatial/) for more formal training options).
